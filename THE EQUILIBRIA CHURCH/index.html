THE EQUILIBRIA CHURCH (TEC)

The word Equilibria is derived from Equilibrium
This means;


  • 1.The condition of a system in which competing influences are balanced resulting in no net change.

  • 2.The state of a body at rest or in a uniform motion in which the resultant of all forces on it is zero.

  • 3.The state of a reaction in which the rates of the forward and reverse reaction are the same.

  • 4.Mental Balance
